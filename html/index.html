Celtic rock

Celtic rock is a hybrid of Irish, Scottish Gaelic, Welsh and Breton styles played on rock instruments. Celtic Rock originated in the 1970s.


Gamelan Music

Gamelan is the traditional ensemble music of Java and Bali in Indonesia, made up predominantly of percussive instruments.

African Music

Music in Africa is very important when it comes to religion. Songs and music are used in rituals and religious ceremonies, to pass down stories from generation to generation, as well as to sing and dance to.
